COBALT INDUSTRIES - A JOINT STATEMENT BY THE COBALT URBAN TRANSIT AUTHORITY AND THE COBALT FORESTRY AND FAUNA INITIATIVE
-----------------------------------------

AS YOU MAY BE AWARE, THE CUTA REPORTED LAST YEAR THAT MULTIPLE CIVILIAN CONTRACTORS HAVE JOINED THE MUTINY, HEADING UNDERGROUND WHERE THE YEAST CAN'T SURVIVE. THE SUBWAY LINES HAVE BEEN INACTIVE SINCE, AND ATTEMPTS TO RECLAIM THE TUNNELS HAVE FAILED. LAST MONTH, THE CFFI BEGAN GETTING REPORTS OF STRANGE ANIMAL BEHAVIOR, INCLUDING CROSS-SPECIES COOPERATION AND SOME WITNESSES REPORTING SUBJECTS HAVE BEGUN DOMESTICATING THE LOCAL WILDLIFE

AFTER SOME WEEKS OF INVESTIGATION, IT BECAME CLEAR THAT THESE ARE IN FACT CONTRACTORS, WHO SEEM TO HAVE ALIGNED WITH THE LOCAL FAUNA, AND SOME OF WHOM SEEM TO HAVE ADAPTED TO THE YEAST. DOCUMENTED CONFLICT BETWEEN THE FAUNA AND THE SUBJECTS SEEMS TO BE ONGOING. TO CLARIFY - THE CONTRACTORS ARE ALIGNED WITH THE FAUNA, AND NEITHER ARE ALIGNED WITH THE SUBJECTS

IT GOES WITHOUT SAYING THAT THIS IS A THREAT TO THE LIFE AND LIMB OF OUR PERSONNEL. AS SUCH, THE SUBWAYS WILL REMAIN CLOSED UNTIL FURTHER NOTICE, AND UNTIL THE CFFI HAS SUCCESSFULLY CULLED THE LOCAL FAUNA, TAKE CARE NOT TO BE CAUGHT UNGUARDED IN THE FORESTS

COBALT INDUSTRIES - ZONING RESTRICTIONS AND MUNICIPAL BYLAWS
-----------------------------------------

WHILE YOU CAN LIVE AMONG SITES OF FORENSIC INTEREST, YOU CANNOT BUILD PERMANENT INFRASTRUCTURE IN THEM. YOUR DUTIES REQUIRE YOU TO LIVE ON THE ISLAND, SO WE UNDERSTAND THAT THERE MAY BE A NEED TO DEPLOY TEMPORARY SHELTER OF SORTS AT THESE SITES

PART OF YOUR DUTIES DURING YOUR CAREER HERE AT COBALT WILL BE THE DEFINITION AND MAPPING OUT OF ZONES OF FORENSIC INTEREST. USING LANTERNS, YOU CAN DEFINE THE ZONE CANDIDATE BEFORE CONFIRMING THE COMPLETION OF THE ZONE MAPPING.

FIREWORKS ARE AN IMPORTANT MEANS OF COMMUNICATION ON THE ISLAND. RED MEANS COME HERE, BLUE MEANS LOCKDOWN, GREEN MEANS GO BACK TO YOUR POST. BOOMERS ARE A SIGNAL TO ALL OF THE AGENTS ON SITE, CANDLES SIGNAL TO ONLY SEND A PORTION, AND VOLCANOS COMMAND YOUR LOCAL SQUAD

TO FORM A SQUAD, TAP AN AGENT WITH A LIT TORCH

USING SKULLS OF SUBJECTS WITH THE FACTOR, PERCHED ON A STICK, SEEMS TO CREATE A FOCAL POINT FOR THE STRANGE LOCALIZED CURVATURE, INCREASING THE ODDS OF REGENERATING THERE. THIS ALSO SERVES TO CLAIM POTENTIAL ZONES OF FORENSIC INTEREST

COBALT INDUSTRIES - LOCAL GOVERNANCE AND CRIMINAL CODE
-----------------------------------------

PER COBALT CORPORATE RESOLUTION 27-1.B, MARTIAL LAW IS INSTITUTED ON THE ISLAND. YOUR ORDER IS TO KILL BOTH SUBJECTS AND CONTRACTORS ON SIGHT.

THE SUBJECTS SEEM TO HAVE ORGANIZED IN SOME SORT OF DECENTRALIZED COMMUNITY. THEY SHOW NO OBVIOUS SIGN OF HIERARCHY, APPARENTLY GOVERNING THROUGH CONSENSUS. THERE IS EVIDENCE OF ONGOING INDUSTRIAL DEVELOPMENT AND TECHNICAL SPECIALIZATION, AND THEIR TECHNOLOGICAL CAPACITY, AIDED BY THE REVERSE ENGINEERING OF SCAVENGED TECHNOLOGY, IS LEADING TO RAPID ADVANCEMENT

THE CONTRACTORS LIVE UNDERGROUND, SHELTERED FROM THE NATIVE YEAST - HAVING ALSO DEFECTED, AND LACKING THE FACTOR THAT GRANTS THE SUBJECTS IMMUNITY, THEY'RE PRETTY MUCH TRAPPED DOWN THERE. SOME OF THEM SEEM TO BE SURFACING, HAVING GAINED IMMUNITY THROUGH UNKNOWN MEANS, AND SEEM TO BE WORKING TO ORGANIZE THE LOCAL FAUNA

THE FACT THAT THE LATTER IS EVEN POSSIBLE DEMANDS FURTHER STUDY. COULD IT BE BECAUSE OF THE REGENERATION PROCESS? WHILE A SYMPTOM IS MEMORY LOSS, NOT EVERYTHING IS LOST IN THE PROCESS. PERHAPS THROUGH CUMULATIVE REGENERATION THEIR COGNITIVE CAPABILITIES HAVE BEEN EXPANDED
